Dynamax

Gigantic power from Galar's Power Spots!

⚡ Dynamax — Complete Guide

🔹 What is Dynamax?

Dynamax is a transformation in which the pokémon grow massive in size and gain access to powerful Max Moves for 3 turns once per battle. Dynamax allows Pokémon to grow to giant sizes, gaining access to powerful Max Moves that can change the course of battle. Gigantamax is a special form of Dynamax that gives certain Pokémon unique appearances and exclusive G-Max Moves. Both mechanics add strategic depth to battles, with players needing to decide when to Dynamax and which moves to use for maximum effect.

🔹 Core Mechanics

  • Only usable in Galar/stadium battles
  • Activates at Power Spots
  • Lasts 3 turns
  • One per battle per Pokémon
  • Normal moves → Max Moves

⚡ Pikachu Dynamax → Max Electric terrain + damage
