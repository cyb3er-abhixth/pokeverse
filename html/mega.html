What is Mega Evolution?

Mega Evolution is a temporary transformation introduced in Pokémon X and Y. It allows certain Pokémon to unlock hidden power during battle using a Mega Stone.

Key Features

  • Only happens during battle
  • Requires a Mega Stone
  • Only one Mega Evolution per battle
  • Massive stat increases
  • Some Pokémon change type
  • Some Pokémon gain new abilities

Mega Evolution Origins

Mega Evolution was discovered in the Kalos region and is believed to be connected to the ultimate weapon fired 3000 years ago during a great war.

Mega Evolution Effects

  • HP stays the same
  • Other stats increase dramatically
  • Abilities can change
  • Types may change
