Z-Moves

Harness the ultimate power of Pokémon with Z-Moves!

⚡ Z-Moves — Complete Guide

🔹 What are Z-Moves?

Z-Moves are powerful, one-time attacks introduced in Pokémon Sun and Moon. They allow a Pokémon to unleash a super-powered version of a move once per battle.

🔹 Core Mechanics

  • Can only be used once per battle
  • Require a Z-Crystal
  • Pokémon must know a matching move
  • Activated via a Z-Ring / Z-Power Ring

⚡ Pikachu + Thunderbolt + Electrium Z → Gigavolt Havoc
