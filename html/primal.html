Primal Reversion

Primal Reversion is an ancient transformation that awakens the original power of Legendary Pokémon. Unlike Mega Evolution, it is a natural form from ancient times, fueled by the energy of the earth and sea.

Ancient Legend

Long ago, Groudon and Kyogre clashed in an epic battle that shaped the world. Groudon expanded the land with its volcanic fury, while Kyogre flooded the oceans with immense tidal power.

When these Pokémon undergo Primal Reversion, they tap into their primal energies, drastically increasing their power and altering the environment during battle.

Legendary Pokémon with Primal Forms

Primal Groudon

Type: Ground / Fire
Ability: Desolate Land
Effect: Summons extremely harsh sunlight, nullifying water attacks.
Base Stats: HP 100 / Attack 180 / Defense 160 / Sp. Atk 150 / Sp. Def 90 / Speed 90

Primal Kyogre

Type: Water
Ability: Primordial Sea
Effect: Summons heavy rain that nullifies fire attacks.
Base Stats: HP 100 / Attack 150 / Defense 90 / Sp. Atk 180 / Sp. Def 160 / Speed 90
