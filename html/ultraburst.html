💥 Ultra Burst

Ultra Burst is a special transformation connected to Necrozma. By absorbing immense light energy, Necrozma changes into a more powerful and radiant form. It is one of the most mysterious and visually stunning mechanics in the Pokémon world.

🌟 What is Ultra Burst?

Ultra Burst is a transformation available to Necrozma after it fuses and reaches a higher power state. It allows Necrozma to become Ultra Necrozma, a legendary dragon-like form overflowing with light.

🧬 Who can use it?

Only Necrozma can use Ultra Burst, specifically after fusing into Dusk Mane Necrozma or Dawn Wings Necrozma.

🔮 Required Item

Ultra Burst requires the special item Ultranecrozium Z. This item allows Necrozma to unleash its hidden light and transform during battle.

🔥 Why is it special?

Unlike many other mechanics, Ultra Burst feels legendary and cosmic. It is tied deeply to story, light, space, and the mystery of Ultra Space.

📖 Ultra Burst Lore

Necrozma is often described as a being that once shone with blinding brilliance. Over time, it lost its light and became incomplete. Its transformation into Ultra Necrozma represents the return of its former glory — an explosion of celestial energy and overwhelming radiance.

This makes Ultra Burst more than just a battle mechanic. It symbolizes restoration, hidden strength, and the awakening of legendary power.
